Unveiling the Mysteries of a Small Planet

Exploring the Artifacts of Culture

On a small planet, as we flew by, artifacts were discovered that were considered by the local civilization to be part of the "Culture".

In an attempt to clarify this concept, and as a result of completing a HTML test task, this website was created. In our analysis of the subject, we explore how we can classify objects of "Ð¡ulture". At first glance, the following aspects can be considered:

Media

The principles cherished by the creators and consumers of the "Culture" were manifested in various forms across different tangible mediums.

Era

We discover cultural artifacts that belong to different temporal segments of the local civilization's existence.

Country

We encounter cultural artifacts scattered across various regions of the planet, excluding those covered by liquid surfaces.

There may be other ways to categorize our findings, but we have not yet devised how to do so.
