Era

We discover cultural artifacts that belong to different temporal segments of the local civilization's existence.

At least, often, by analyzing the depth of their burial, the half-life period of radioactive elements, or just of how it looks, it is possible to determine when the artifacts were created.

Books

Books are when conceptual structures are first expressed in a sequence of commonly accepted symbols (words), then the sound analogues of these words are broken down into a sequence of sounds, and then the sounds are represented by graphical symbols. Afterwards, chains of symbols are sequentially recorded.

Music

The population found pleasure in the elusive patterns of sound sequences. Apparently, such sounds were intentionally recorded and then repeatedly played.

Cinema
